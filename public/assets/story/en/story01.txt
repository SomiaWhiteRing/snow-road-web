I am now

inside you

I left home and left the town,

and alone, I wander within you

And yet,

still, I cannot hear your voice

#cg scene_self01.bmp
No matter how deeply I breathe in,
no matter how deeply I breathe out,

you do nothing but pass through my body,

and I have not been granted
even a single word.
I can think of no other conclusion.

You, who fall upon every part
of this land at the farthest north,

you, who alight quietly
and lie wherever the eye can reach,

in the end, I could not believe in you.

Fuleuden, the people
who let snow and heart commune.

We have listened for your voice
and bowed our heads before your greatness,

but to hear your words,
I alone could not do it,
not even once.

#cg scene_self00.bmp
Why will you not grant me your voice?

Do you hate me?
Can you not see me either? Can you not hear my voice?

This question that still remains in my chest,

does it no longer
even matter to ask?

Can I no longer

remain one of the people of snow?

Can I no longer love you?

Can I not even fear you?
