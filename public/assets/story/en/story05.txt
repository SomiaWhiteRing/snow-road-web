Before my eyes stood
a large cylindrical structure.

There was no entrance,

and smoke kept billowing from above.

This facility is called
the <<Furnace>>.

Inside it,
several million fire spirits are confined,

and within the sealed space
they go on quarreling without end.

Magic circles are drawn on the inner wall,
and only heat is apportioned and transferred
to the many magic circles in the town.

It is a precious energy source of Fuleuden,
but fearing an outbreak,
it was built here, far from the town.

I...

I had been aiming for this place.

I came here
to meet the fire within it.
