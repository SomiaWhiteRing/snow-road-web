In the frozen sea, there was a girl.

The girl was sealed inside the ice,
unable to move.

#cg scene_fish.bmp
And yet, as though she were playing with
the fish frozen beside her,

the girl's face was smiling.

As if this very moment were heaven itself,
that was the scene before me.

Surely the girl

had known she would freeze.

Surely

it was what she herself had wished for.

Even now,

she must still be receiving
that blissful moment in her body.

The whole sea
froze in a single instant.

And that within it there had been

more lives than even God could count,

the girl, too,

must surely have known.
