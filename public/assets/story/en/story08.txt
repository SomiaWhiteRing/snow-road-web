343 years ago...

there was a girl.

When she was young,
she suffered terrible burns over her entire body.

It was an unforeseen accident.

It was pure chance.

It was no one's malice.

She could not hate other people,
nor her parents,
nor her friends.

She could only hate
the fire,
the heat itself,
that had wounded her.

Her hatred was fierce and baleful.
If the mind could be made visible,
it would have looked like flame itself, and yet --

never noticing that contradiction,
she shut her savage anger away
inside an unwitting box.

Eventually she learned magic
and mastered the control of water and cold.

She learned greedily.

She learned without limit.

She looked well upon
what people did not wish to see,

and even upon
what people ought not to see...

And at the very end,
that ugly body marked by burns,

she sealed it
inside a crystal of ice.

By means of that ritual,
she cast away the flesh,

sublimated into cold itself,

rose into the sky
and became a tornado of snow,

danced in frenzy,

and then

covered this star.

Unending cold
and a pure white darkness
came over the world.

At length...

the sea froze.

While still swallowing
the lives that had seemed never to diminish...
