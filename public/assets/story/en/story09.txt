I had been repeating reincarnation.

#cg scene_self07.bmp
Each time I passed through a dream
and received a new life,
my memories grew dimmer.

I had lost
the information concerning
my soul's past.

I did not know
how I had come to be.

But...

But I never wavered.

Consistently, always,
I kept thinking,
"It's cold."
That much was certain.

The oldest memory
that remains

is the sight of this planet,

covered over by ice,

turned into something like
a snowball,

and the fierce sense of wrongness
toward that state.

I could not forgive it.

Snow, always clinging to me,
pressing despair into my core...

it was enough to hate.

I always wished
to shake it off.

#cg scene_self08.bmp
All the while enduring it,
I kept examining...

...what was the cause
of this abnormal climate.

I even threw myself
into the snowfields to investigate.

And I obtained information
at the level of the sky.

Judging from the atmospheric composition,
the conditions for an ice age
were nowhere near being met.

This planet's thermal veil
was still intact!

It was obvious
that this phenomenon
had an artificial cause.

#cg scene_self09.bmp
So,
in order to make contact with the snow,

I reincarnated
as one of the people of Fuleuden.

Since before the glacial age,
they had lived in a land of snow,

and were skilled
at communicating with it.

Their closed culture
was hard to grow used to
and brought me pain,
but I kept telling myself
that it was only this once,
and somehow endured.

No one realized
that I was an outsider.

But despite going that far,
it was a miscalculation
that I still could not
make contact with the snow.

No matter how many times
I attempted Fuleuding,
the snow rejected me every time.

Unable to learn
what I had to know,
I panicked.

And wandered the snowfields.

#cg scene_self10.bmp
After lying with that man,
I learned what I was.

My spirit
was akin to flame.

No wonder
the snow rejected me.

By becoming conscious
of my own nature,
I was able
to accustom my body as well
to fire.

I visited the <<Furnace>>,
made contact with the aged flames there,
and learned the truth.

And I remembered
my mission as well.

It was
to assassinate the magician
who was the core of this cold.

I came here
to kill Coldecot...

"Goodbye."

I brought the axe down.

And so
my ideal was reflected into the world.

The endless spread of matter,

stillness and motion
and motion and stillness,
a prosperity of combinations
too many for even God to count,

the tiny lives breathing within it,
the hearts dwelling there,
the feelings they feel,

the great swell of information
they spin,

worlds divided by size and speed
and isolated from one another,

histories that repeat in vain,
never reaching anyone,

if flame is what devours corpses,
then flame is also
what calms them...
